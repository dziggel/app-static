Accreditation

GO University received its operational licence from the National Universities Commission (NUC) in 2009, and so commenced full academic activities in the 2009/2010. All our programmes have NUC accreditation. GO University’s law faculty also has the approval of the Legal Council of Education. GO University also runs postgraduate programmes in Education, Management and Social Sciences, Natural and Applied Sciences. We have produced graduates who were all successfully mobilized for the National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) scheme in their year of graduation.
